Appendix/Sensors & Such

Using your LCD with Arduino

The Library you need for your LCD is included with Arduino. You should open File - Examples - LiquidCrystal - HelloWorld

Try some of the other example sketches to see how this works. Once you are done see oif you can use the LCD to dsiplay other stuff: - Hook up the DHT11 and display the temperature and humidity - Attach your Ultrasonic range finder and display the distance away. - Hook up some buttons and make a simple game
